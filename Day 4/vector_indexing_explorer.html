N vectors sit in the search space. The red dot is your query. Right now we know nothing about them.
tiny math
A vector is a list of numbers. Closer vectors = more similar meaning.
Vector
A list of numbers (e.g. 1,536 of them) that represents the meaning of a text chunk.
Distance
How different two vectors are. Closer = more similar in meaning.
Top-k
The k vectors closest to the query. Typically k = 5 or 10.
Recall
Of the true top-k, how many did the index actually find? 100% = perfect.
